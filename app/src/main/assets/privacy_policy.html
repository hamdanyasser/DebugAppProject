DebugMaster Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Introduction

DebugMaster ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Progress Data: Your learning progress, scores, achievements, and streak data.
  • Purchase Information: If you subscribe to DebugMaster Pro, Google Play handles all payment processing. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but not your payment details.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used and time spent.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Analytics: Anonymous analytics to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app functionality
  • To track your learning progress and achievements
  • To process your subscription purchases
  • To send you important updates about the app
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To respond to your support requests

Data Storage

Your progress data is stored locally on your device. If you create an account, your data may be synced to our secure cloud servers to enable cross-device access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that help us operate the app (analytics, cloud storage)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your progress data
  • Opt out of analytics collection

Children's Privacy

DebugMaster is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@debugmaster.app

Google Play Requirements

This app complies with Google Play's Developer Program Policies and User Data policy.
