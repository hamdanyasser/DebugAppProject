# Privacy Policy for DebugMaster

**Last Updated: November 2024**

## Introduction

DebugMaster ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
- **Progress Data**: Your learning progress, completed challenges, achievements, and XP.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent, and performance data.

### Payment Information
- Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment card details.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the app
- Save and sync your learning progress
- Process subscriptions and purchases
- Send notifications about your progress
- Improve our app and user experience
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your progress is stored locally on your device.
- **Cloud Sync**: If you sign in, progress may be synced to our secure servers.
- **Retention**: We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Service Providers**: Cloud hosting and analytics services that help us operate the app.
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights.

## Children's Privacy

DebugMaster is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt out of marketing communications

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure data transmission.

## Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services:

- **Firebase**: For analytics and crash reporting (if enabled)

These services have their own privacy policies.
